What is a financial market?

A financial market is any marketplace where the the buying and selling of securities occurs, including the stock market, bond market, forex market, and derivatives market, among others.

Examples of financial markets include the New York Stock Exchange (shares), the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (commodities), and the forex market.

Investing vs. trading

Investing is a long-term form of financial trading which involves buying and holding financial assets over a number of months or years. In contrast, trading (aka speculation). Active traders tend to focus on short-term market movements, with some participants placing hundreds of trades per day.
